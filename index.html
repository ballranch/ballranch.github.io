Our Products

Our Honey Collection

Our raw honey is harvested with care, minimally filtered to preserve beneficial enzymes and pollen, and never heated above natural hive temperatures.

  • Wildflower Honey: A rich, complex blend reflecting our local ecosystem's diverse flora
  • Seasonal Variety Honey: Distinctive flavor notes from specific flower sources
  • Special Reserve Honey: Our special offering with unique characteristics

Each jar is bottled with the same precision and care that defined our military service.

Bee Products

Beyond honey, our bees provide remarkable substances we craft into:

  • Pure Beeswax Candles: Hand-poured, long-burning, with a subtle honey aroma
  • Propolis Tincture: Supporting immune function with bee-collected plant resins
  • Bee Pollen: Nature's complete superfood, collected fresh from our hives
  • Honey-Based Skincare: Simple, natural products for radiant skin

Goat Companions

While our farm focuses primarily on bee products, our goats are beloved farm members who:

  • Help maintain our land through grazing
  • Provide companionship and joy
  • Serve as ambassadors during farm educational events
  • Remind us daily of the importance of gentle stewardship
