About Us

Our Story

Goats 'n Honey Haven was born from the intersection of service and stewardship. After serving 12 years in the U.S. Army as a logistics specialist with two deployments to Afghanistan, founder Emily Martinez found healing and purpose through beekeeping and raising goats.

As a service-connected veteran and woman farmer, Emily brings unique perspective and precision to agricultural practices. The attention to detail and systems thinking from her military logistics background has proven invaluable in managing healthy beehives and creating sustainable farm operations.

What began with two beehives and a pair of goats in 2018 has grown into a thriving farm that honors military values of discipline, resilience, and attention to detail. Today, Emily leads a small team including fellow veterans who have found purpose and healing through agricultural work.

Our Promise

  • Operating with the integrity and dedication instilled through military service
  • Practicing sustainable beekeeping that prioritizes bee health and biodiversity
  • Providing ethical care for our goat companions
  • Producing premium, small-batch honey and bee products using traditional methods
  • Creating educational opportunities for our community about pollinators and sustainable farming
  • Supporting fellow veterans through agriculture and donating 5% of profits to veteran support programs
  • Employing natural hive management using essential oils instead of harsh chemicals

Our Farm

Nestled in the rolling hills of Western Pennsylvania, our 15-acre farm creates an ideal environment for both our bees and goats. Our apiary consists of 48 hives strategically positioned to take advantage of local wildflowers, native plants, and our own pollinator gardens, resulting in distinctively flavorful honey with regional terroir. Meanwhile, our small herd of Nigerian Dwarf goats enjoy spacious pastures and excellent care.

We employ sustainable practices across our operation, including:

  • Chemical-free hive management using organic methods and essential oils
  • Natural goat care protocols with herbal supplements
  • Water conservation techniques including rainwater collection
  • Native pollinator plantings covering 3 acres of dedicated habitat
  • Season extension through passive solar techniques
  • Eco-friendly packaging using recycled and biodegradable materials

Our Honey Collection

Our raw honey is harvested with care using military-grade precision and attention to detail. Every jar is minimally filtered to preserve beneficial enzymes and pollen, and never heated above natural hive temperatures. We practice a "hive to table" philosophy, ensuring maximum nutritional benefits and authentic flavor profiles.

Wildflower Honey

A rich, complex blend reflecting our local ecosystem's diverse flora from native goldenrod to clover and basswood. Harvested in small batches throughout the season to capture unique seasonal characteristics.
